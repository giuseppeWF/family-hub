📋 Privacy Policy
What we store
Family events, to-dos, shopping lists, meal plans, household tasks, and saved favourites. Family member names and colours are kept locally on this device.
Where it lives
All data is stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) on a project you control. Nothing is sent to any other service.
Who can see it
Anyone with the URL to your Family Hub can view and edit data. Keep the link within your family.
Tracking & ads
None. No cookies, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party data sharing.
Deleting your data
Use the "Delete all family data" button in Settings → Privacy & Data to permanently remove everything from Firestore and this device.
