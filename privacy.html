Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Family Hub is a private family application. It is not a public service. All data is stored in a Firebase Firestore database controlled by your family administrator.

1. Who we are

Family Hub is operated by the Lucarelli family for private use. Contact: mrglucarelli@gmail.com

For GDPR purposes, the family administrator (Giuseppe Lucarelli) is the data controller.

3. Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

Processing is based on legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — specifically the legitimate interest of a family unit in organising shared schedules, tasks, and household management. All data subjects are members of the same family who have consented by using the application.

4. How data is stored and protected

  • All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in Google Cloud (europe-west region where configured).
  • Firebase applies encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Access to the application is restricted by a shared URL and PIN code.
  • The Firebase API key is restricted to the family-hub domain (see S5-006).
  • Firestore security rules restrict write access to valid data structures.
  • Full per-user authentication (Google Sign-In) is planned in a future release (S5-003).

5. Data retention

  • Deleted items are soft-deleted (marked hidden, not removed) for 30 days, then permanently erased.
  • Activity log entries are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • All other data is retained indefinitely unless the family administrator deletes it.

6. Third parties

Family Hub uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — database and hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy. A Data Processing Agreement is available through Google Cloud Console.
  • Open-Meteo — weather data (temperature and conditions for Bury, Manchester). No personal data is sent. Open-Meteo Terms.
  • Google Fonts — Nunito and Inter typefaces loaded from Google CDN. No personal data is sent beyond standard HTTP request headers.

No data is sold, shared with advertisers, or processed for analytics by any third party.

7. Your rights (UK GDPR)

As a data subject you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data (contact the family administrator)
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data (edit items directly in the app)
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data. Use "Delete all family data" in Settings, or contact the administrator.
  • Restriction — request that processing be paused
  • Portability — request your data in a structured format
  • Object — object to processing on legitimate interests grounds

To exercise any right, contact: mrglucarelli@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies and tracking

Family Hub uses no cookies, no analytics, no advertising tracking, and no third-party pixels. The only browser storage used is localStorage for device-specific preferences (device user name, hub name cache, UI state).

9. Children

Family Hub is a private family application used by family members including children (under 18). All data belongs to the family unit and is controlled by the family administrator. No children's data is shared with third parties or used for any purpose other than family coordination.

10. Deleting your data

To permanently delete all family data:

  1. Open Family Hub
  2. Tap the ⚙️ settings icon (PIN required)
  3. Scroll to Privacy & Data
  4. Tap Delete all family data
  5. Confirm the deletion

This permanently removes all data from Firestore and clears localStorage on the current device. Other devices will reload to an empty state.

11. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of Family Hub constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
